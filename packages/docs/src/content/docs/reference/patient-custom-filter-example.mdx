This example uses only custom filters and custom data input views to express patient field-to-field checks. It does not rely on preset cross-field filters or expression inputs.

The dataset includes patient ID, admission and discharge dates, admission and discharge systolic blood pressure, and admission and discharge weight.

Useful filters to try:

- `date field is days before another date field`: admission date is 1 to 14 days before discharge date
- `numeric field is less than another field`: discharge systolic is less than admission systolic
- `numeric field absolute difference is at most`: discharge weight differs from admission weight by at most 5
